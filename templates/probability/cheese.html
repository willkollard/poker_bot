There are 1326 ways to deal 2 cards. This is equal to (52*51)/2 (as you could get Ace spades and King spades or King spades then Ace spades - 52*51 takes counts both AceSpades/KingSpades or KingSpades/AceSpades when in reality it's only one) or its equal to 52C2.

In Texas Hold'em, position is a huge advantage - you want to be as close to the Button as possible (as the Button the last person to act after the flop). This is called the late position.
